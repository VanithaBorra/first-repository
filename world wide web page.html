world wide web

The WorldWideWeb (W3) is a wide-area hypermedia. information retrieval initiative aiming to give universal access to a large universe of documents.

Everything there is online about W3 is linked directly or indirectly to this document, including an excutive summary of the project, mailing lists, Policy , November's WEB news , Frequently Asked Questions.
